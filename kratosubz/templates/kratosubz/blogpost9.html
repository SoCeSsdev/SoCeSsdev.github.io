How to Pay Electricity Bills Without Stress

By KratoSubz Team

Gone are the days of queuing at PHCN offices or struggling to find vendors to pay your electricity bill. With KratoSubz, you can now pay for prepaid and postpaid electricity services online — fast, easy, and stress-free.

Why Pay Your Electricity Bills Online?

  • Instant delivery of prepaid tokens
  • Secure payment process
  • Available 24/7 – even on weekends
  • No more queues or bank visits
  • Supports all major electricity distribution companies (DisCos) in Nigeria

Supported Electricity Providers

KratoSubz supports both prepaid and postpaid services for major DisCos including:

  • IKEDC (Ikeja Electric)
  • EKEDC (Eko Electric)
  • BEDC (Benin Disco)
  • PHEDC (Port Harcourt)
  • KEDCO, JED, AEDC, and more

How to Pay Your Electricity Bill on KratoSubz

1. Log in to Your Account

Go to KratoSubz login and access your dashboard. New user? Sign up here.

2. Fund Your Wallet

Use bank transfer or debit card to top up your wallet balance. This is the amount used to make utility payments.

3. Choose “Electricity Bill”

On your dashboard, select the Electricity option. Choose your DisCo, then select either Prepaid or Postpaid.

4. Enter Meter Details

Input your meter number, name, phone number, and amount to be paid. Ensure your details are accurate to avoid errors.

5. Confirm and Pay

Review your payment summary and confirm. You’ll receive your prepaid token or postpaid confirmation immediately.

Helpful Tips

  • For prepaid meters, ensure your meter is active and not tampered with
  • Always cross-check meter number before submitting payment
  • Use your dashboard to download receipts for proof
