What is Data Reselling?

Data reselling involves purchasing internet data bundles at discounted reseller rates and selling them to individuals or groups for a profit. You can sell to students, office workers, business owners, or entire communities.

Why You Should Start Data Reselling in Nigeria

  • Low capital requirement
  • Consistent demand from all age groups
  • Instant profit per transaction
  • Can be run from a smartphone, no shop needed
  • Flexible — part-time or full-time
